Pizza

Description

Who does not like pizza? Pizza is probably the best kind of food to eat with friends and family. I belive that pizza is probably one of the best food that humans ever made.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • meat

Steps to make it

  1. Make some dough
  2. Put tomato on the dough
  3. Put cheese on the tomato
  4. Put meat on the cheese
  5. Put the pizza for the oven for 5 minutes until it is ready
