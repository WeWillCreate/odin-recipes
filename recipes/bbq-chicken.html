BBQ Chicken

Description

As you can see here there is bbq chicken. It is looks very tasty and the best thing about this that it looks like this in real life too. I find it to be amazing and its probably the best kind of chicken to eat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Grill
  • Butter

Steps to make it

  1. Mix the chicken with the bbq sauce
  2. Put melted butter over the chicken
  3. Marinate the chicken
  4. Put the chicken on the grill
  5. Wait until the chicken is giving a brown and black color
